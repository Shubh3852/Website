About Shubh.

    A writer and a storyteller Shubham Agrawal (Shubh), born on September 19, is a young boy from Bokaro, Jharkhand who is currently pursuing B.Tech from Gandhi Institute of Engineering & Technology, Gunupur, Odisha. He loves writing stories and listen to peoples. He learn life in a very practical way. He is normally very reserved with a handful of friends and most of the time, live life alone and always prepared to help others. He is highly disciplined, persistence and encourageous, and is the only strength thats taking him to success. He is a great part of a family team and very challenging, once he make a decision, he keep it that way for a long time.
